About Me:

My Name is Brendan Hood and I am a Computer Science student attending the University of Maryland. I began my time at UMD studying Computer Engineering in the A. James Clark School of Engineering. Last semester, I shifted focus towards a more pure Computer Science major as it better aligned with my interests.

As of late, I have been interested in web design and implementation, however in time would like to get into topics such as Machine Learning and Solutions Architecture.

I fully researched, designed, and implemented everything for this website from Jan. 12-13 2021. the current version is pure HTML/CSS for learning purposes but will be updated with JS and a higher-level CSS framework in short time.

I also serve as the President of the Maryland Beta chapter of Sigma Alpha Epsilon.

I have learned invaluable leadership skills during my time of presiding over my chapter over 70 guys and joining a base of hundreds of MD-Beta brothers who have joined since our founding in 1943.
